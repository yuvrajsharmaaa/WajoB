### Common Questions

**Q: How much does WajoB cost to use?**  
A: WajoB is free! You only pay blockchain gas fees (typically 0.01-0.05 TON per transaction).

**Q: What if the worker doesn't complete the job?**  
A: Escrow has a deadline. If not completed, funds auto-refund to employer.

**Q: Is my money safe in escrow?**
